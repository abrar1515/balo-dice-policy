Balo Dice Game - Account Deletion Policy

Developer: Balo Dice Game Team

How to request account deletion

If you want to delete your account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: balodicegame@gmail.com
  2. Use subject: Account Deletion Request - Balo Dice Game
  3. Include your registered Google email used in the game

Data that will be deleted

  • Google login user profile
  • Game progress and level data
  • Coins, rewards, and in-game items

Data that may be retained

  • Some data may be kept for security and fraud prevention
  • Retention period: up to 30 days

Processing time

Account deletion requests are usually processed within 7–14 days.
